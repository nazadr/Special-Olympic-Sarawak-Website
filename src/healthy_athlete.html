Healthy Athletes Program

The Special Olympics Healthy Athletes program provides free health screenings and education to Special Olympics athletes in a welcoming, fun environment. Our goal is to improve athletes' ability to train and compete in Special Olympics.

Our Health Disciplines

The Healthy Athletes program offers health screenings in these key areas:

FunFitness

Physical therapy screenings assess flexibility, strength, balance, and aerobic fitness. Athletes receive exercise recommendations to improve their sports performance.

Healthy Hearing

Screenings identify possible hearing loss, ear wax buildup, or need for follow-up care. Many athletes receive hearing aids for the first time.

Special Smiles

Dental screenings check for gum disease, tooth decay, and other oral health problems. Athletes receive preventive education and care recommendations.

Opening Eyes

Vision screenings test visual acuity and check for eye diseases. Thousands of athletes receive free prescription eyewear each year.

Fit Feet

Podiatric screenings assess foot and ankle problems that may affect athletes' mobility and performance. Many receive orthotics or referrals for further care.

Health Promotion

Education on nutrition, sun safety, hydration, and healthy lifestyle choices to improve overall wellbeing.
