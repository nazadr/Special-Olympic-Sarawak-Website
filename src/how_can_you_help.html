How You Can Help
Change Lives

Your support creates opportunities and celebrates abilities. Together, we can build a more inclusive world for children with intellectual disabilities.

1

Identify and Involve

Help us identify and get involved with children who are 8 years and above with intellectual disabilities. Your awareness and support can make a significant difference in their lives by connecting them with the resources and community they need to thrive.

2

Volunteer Your Skills

Give your time and expertise in whatever field you're available. Whether it's teaching, sports coaching, arts, or administrative support, your volunteer work directly impacts the lives of individuals with intellectual disabilities.

3

Donate Generously

Provide financial support or in-kind gifts to help us sustain and expand our programs. Your donations enable us to offer more services, activities, and resources to individuals with intellectual disabilities and their families.

4

Enlist Your Organization

Engage your service club or organization in our movement. Together we can advocate more effectively for the needs and rights of people with intellectual disabilities, creating positive change at a systemic level.

5

Contribute Your Time

Spend time at your local activities or sporting venues supporting individuals with intellectual disabilities. Your presence and encouragement helps create an inclusive environment where everyone can participate and enjoy sports and activities.

Will you join us in this special journey?

We can only make a difference if you care. Every small action creates ripples of change in the lives of children with intellectual disabilities. Your support today can change someone's tomorrow.
