Why Join Special Olympics?

Skill Development

Develop athletic skills, confidence, and independence through sports training and competition.

Community

Join a welcoming community of athletes, families, volunteers, and supporters.

Joy & Celebration

Experience the spirit of sportsmanship, achievement, and personal best.

Health Benefits

Improved physical fitness, emotional well-being, and quality of life.

Leadership

Opportunities to develop leadership skills and take on roles in athlete leadership programs.

Global Network

Be part of a worldwide movement in over 190 countries.
