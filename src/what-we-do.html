About Special Olympics

Special Olympics is a global movement that provides year-round sports training and athletic competition in a variety of Olympic-type sports for children and adults with intellectual disabilities. Our mission is to create a better world by fostering the acceptance and inclusion of all people.

  • Empowering athletes to develop physical fitness, demonstrate courage, and experience joy.
  • Promoting understanding, acceptance, and inclusion between people with and without intellectual disabilities.
  • Offering health screenings, leadership opportunities, and family support.
  • Building a supportive community through sports, education, and advocacy.

Our Programs

  • Sports Training & Competition: Year-round training and events in athletics, swimming, football, and more.
  • Unified Sports: Teams made up of people with and without intellectual disabilities playing together.
  • Healthy Athletes: Free health screenings and education for athletes.
  • Young Athletes: Early childhood play program for children ages 2-7.
  • Family & Community: Support and engagement for families and volunteers.

Through these programs, Special Olympics Sarawak transforms lives, builds confidence, and inspires hope. Join us in making a difference!
